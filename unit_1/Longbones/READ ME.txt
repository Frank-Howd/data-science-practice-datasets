Researchers sampled long bone material from bodies exhumed from coffin burials in two cemeteries in England. 
In each case, date of death and burial (and therefore interment time) was known.  Data stored in Longbones.csv.


Source: D.R. Jarvis (1997). "Nitrogen Levels in Long Bones from Coffin Burials Interred for Periods of 26-90 Years," 
Forensic Science International, Vol85, pp199-208

Data Dictionary:

Variable Name	   	Description	        		Details
Site	             	Site ID					Character [A, LG].  It is not reported what these abbreviations stand for
Time			Interment time				Numeric [years]
Depth			Burial depth				Numeric [feet]
Lime			Quicklime application at burial		Numeric[0,1] 0 = no, 1 = yes
Age			Age at time of death			Numeric [years]  Unknown for some bodies
Nitro			Nitrogen level				Numeric [g/100g of bone]
Oil			Oil contamination			Numeric [0,1] 0 = no, 1 = yes
