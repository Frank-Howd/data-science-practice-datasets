The Brainhead.csv dataset provides information on 237 individuals who were subject to post-mortem examination at the 
Middlesex Hospital in London around the turn of the 20th century. 

Source: R.J. Gladstone (1905). "A Study of the Relations of the Brain to the Size of the Head", 
Biometrika, Vol. 4, pp105-123.
